Welcome to Trapper's Ridge Bed & Breakfast!

Nestled in the heart of tranquility, where pillows become clouds and breakfast is more than just a meal—it’s an experience. At Trapper's Ridge, we don’t just offer you a place to rest your head; we invite you to escape the ordinary and embrace the extraordinary.

Imagine waking up to the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the sound of birds chirping outside your window. Our charming rooms are designed to whisk you away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life, offering a retreat where relaxation reigns supreme.

But it’s not just about the fluffy pillows and soft linens (though we’ve got those in abundance!), it’s about the little touches that make your stay truly unforgettable. From our homemade breakfasts crafted with love to our cozy nooks perfect for curling up with a good book, every moment at Trapper's Ridge is designed to delight.

And let’s not forget about our resident furry friends—our resident cats, Whiskers and Snickers, are always on hand to provide some purr therapy and extra cuddles.

So, whether you’re seeking a romantic getaway, a solo retreat, or a fun-filled family adventure, come join us at Trapper's Ridge Bed & Breakfast, where every stay is a dream come true. Book now and let the magic begin!
